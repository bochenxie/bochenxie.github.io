Biography

     I obtained the Ph.D. degree from the City University of Hong Kong (CityUHK), Hong Kong SAR, China, in 2024, with the supervision of Prof. Youfu Li. Previously, I received the B.Eng. degree from the China University of Petroleum, Qingdao, China, in 2019 and the M.Sc. degree from CityUHK in 2020. I work in the field of computer vision and deep learning, and focus on the following research topics:
  • Multi-Modal Learning => Multi-Modal Alignment and Fusion, Cross-Modal Transfer Learning, and MLLMs
  • Visual Perception with Event Cameras => Representation, Learning, and Downstream Tasks
  • Multimedia Forensics => AIGC, Forgery Detection, and Information Forensics
  • Model Compression => Knowledge Distillation and Network Pruning
